# Spa Landing Page Design

## Goal

Build a luxury Korean-inspired Vietnamese landing page for Phùng Hà KOREA that turns Facebook traffic into qualified consultation leads for facial care, melasma/dullness, acne marks, pores, and at-home face-lifting/build-face routines.

## Positioning

The page is a 30-second skin consultation test, not a generic shop brochure and not a free-form AI chatbot. Visitors answer a short quiz, receive a safe routine suggestion, then send the result code back to the fanpage/Messenger for a real seller to verify.

## Audience

Primary visitors are women in their 20s, 30s, and 40s who came from Facebook and may be skeptical of unknown domains. The page should build trust quickly by showing the fanpage identity, Bac Ninh/Tien Du location, phone number, Korean domestic skincare positioning, and soft non-medical language.

## Structure

1. Hero with clear CTA: "Tư vấn routine làm mặt, nám/sạm & da căng bóng trong 30 giây."
2. Trust strip with fanpage signals.
3. Five-question quiz covering skin type, concern, product history, budget, and goal.
4. Result card with routine title, reason list, morning/evening usage, price range, and Messenger CTA.
5. Need-based service cards for melasma/dullness, acne/pores, and build-face/glow routines.
6. Explanation section stating this is a need-filtering flow for human consultation.
7. Policy and FAQ with safe answers.

## Visual Direction

Luxury/refined Korean spa: ivory porcelain background, champagne highlights, rose-clay accents, deep pine contrast, editorial serif headlines, soft glass cards, rounded arch hero imagery, and subtle grid texture. The design should feel premium and Asian/Korean without looking like a generic purple SaaS landing page.

## Safety and Claims

Use "hỗ trợ cải thiện", "dưỡng sáng", "phục hồi", and "shop kiểm tra lại" language. Avoid claims like "trị khỏi", "xóa nám tận gốc", "cam kết hết mụn", or medical certainty.
